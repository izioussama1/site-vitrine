About Us

TAKE CHARGE OF YOUR HEALTH: ABOUT IHEALTH

iHealth is dedicated to helping people lead healthier lives.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for individuals of all ages to take a more active role in managing their health.

OUR APPROACH

We do this by designing and manufacturing innovative, consumer-friendly, mobile personal healthcare products that connect to the cloud.

iHealth products are easy to use, making it simple for consumers to accurately measure, track, and share a full range of health vitals.

By automatically connecting the data through the cloud, consumers are able to see a more comprehensive view of their vitals and easily share information with healthcare professionals or loved ones.
